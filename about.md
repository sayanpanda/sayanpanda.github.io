I am a PhD student in Astrophysics at the [**Indian Institute of Astrophysics** (IIA)](https://www.iiap.res.in), Bangalore, India. My doctoral research focuses on the interstellar medium, with a particular emphasis on dust polarization and magnetic fields in high lattitude clouds. 

## Background

I completed my masters in Physics from [**National Institute of Technology Rourkela**](https://www.nitrkl.ac.in/) before joining IIA for my doctoral studies.

## Research Interests

- Structure and dynamics of the interstellar medium
- Dust grain alignment theory and polarimetry
- Magnetic field morphology in molecular clouds
- Multi-wavelength observational astrophysics
- Statistical methods for polarization analysis

## Beyond Research

When I am not at the telescope or writing code, I enjoy reading non-fiction books, photography and exploring the intersections of science and philosophy.
